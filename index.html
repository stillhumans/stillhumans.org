The Project

We meet syrian refugees in Stockholm.

They get disposable cameras to capture their situation.

We collect the cameras after 3 days.

We exhibit their stories.

About

Still Humans is a project created by 5 students from Hyper Island, a digital business school focusing on real-world industry training using digital technology. We have been challenged by an industry leader to help the refugee crisis, using only code and our own creativity. In the very beginning, we decided that our main aim should be to fight dehumanization and tackle the stigma around the refugees.

We recognized two things: 1. We only meet the refugees through the media's narrative. 2.The media's coverage of the Syrian war and its refugees stayed outside of Swedish borders. By giving cameras directly to the people, we believe that we will get closer to their everyday reality. We desire that these pictures to be the start of a tolerant society. We desire that these pictures will remind about that refugees are more than distant foreign people in war-pictures. We desire that these pictures will remind you about that the refugees are still humans.
